What is Textnook?

TextNook is an online marketplace for connecting buyers (individuals/businesses who look to rent or buy) with sellers (individual/business who offer the books for rent/sale) across India.

How does TextNook work?

If you need any book, you can either browse our categories or search for the product. After selecting the product(s) you will be able to see the relevant sellers who are ready to sell or rent those books. After selecting to rent or buy, you will be directed to fill the shipping and payment details.

If you wish to post any book for sale/rent, use our “sell/rent books” feature, upload the details about the book you want to sell/rent etc.

Who benefits from TextNook?

Students are the primary beneficiaries from TextNook. We are dedicated to help them save money by providing the choice of buying/renting new or used books at affordable prices.

Also students can choose to rent/sell their used textbooks.

Small businesses can also sell on TextNook, and they can reach across India are no longer limited by their geographical location.

The book shop owners can cater to the demand from students across India, thus they no longer may have to carry a pile of unsold textbooks in their shops/warehouses.

I have noticed that you have been referring to textbooks in good condition throughout your website. Can you elucidate what “good condition” means?

We hold ourselves to high quality standards while selecting the textbooks that we offer for rent or buy on our website.

A textbook in "good condition" is always free from the following:

• Stains

• Water damage

• Missing, loose, or torn pages/cover

• Excessive writing/highlighting

• Torn or damaged binding

• Any other significant damage or disfigurement

It can have minor signs of wear and tear such as the following:

• Some writing/highlighting

• Rounded or dog-eared corners

• Minor scrapes or scratches

• Faded cover art

• Creased pages

Also, the book has to have all the supplementary items such as original CDs/DVDs, work books or any other forms of study aids.

What type of payment methods do you accept for purchases?

We offer the following payment options:

• Credit/debit cards issued by all major banks

• Net banking

• COD(Cash on Delivery)

What kind of textbooks can I rent/buy?

All the professional textbooks that are part of the syllabus of any Indian University recognized by UGC. All professional streams such as Medical, Engineering, Agricultural, Legal and Accounting, Finance, etc from all major publishers.

How long can I keep a book on rent?

You can keep the books till the rent duration specified by the seller in the listing.

Can I buy the textbook I rented?

If you don’t attempt to return the book at the end of rental period, it is assumed that you want to keep the book for yourself and you will not get any cash back, that you would have got in case you returned the books on time.

How do I know whether the book that is listed on TextNook is new or used?

For any book listed on our site, the sellers mention the details about the condition of the book.

I don’t find the book I want in the search results. What do I do?

In case you don’t find the book you want, you are provided an option of adding the details of that book. We will try to get that particular book for you.

Can I sell/rent books other than textbooks?

As of now, TextNook is dedicated towards providing an exclusive platform for textbooks. So, you can only offer textbooks for sale/rent on our platform.

Do I need to pay any registration or listing fees?

No, TextNook does not charge any registration or listing fees from any of its sellers. You will only be charged a nominal fee on any transaction you carry out.

Can I sell or rent used books?

Yes, you can sell or rent the used books, but the books should be in a good condition. Also, it is mandatory to specify the condition of the book (new/used, any damages etc) while you list it on TextNook.

Can I only choose to sell my books, without offering for rent?

Yes, you can choose either to sell or rent or you can opt for both sell/rent for any of the books you list on TextNook.

Can I cancel my account?

You can cancel your account, after settling (if any) pending deliveries or payments.

What are the shipping charges?

We intend to make online shopping experience seamless. You may be charged a flat fee per item you order or avail free shipping offered by some sellers if you shop with them for a minimum predefined value. Any delivery charges will be mentioned by the sellers against the item’s listing on TextNook.

If you are returning the book(s) before seven (7) days for a full refund, then the shipping expenses have to be borne by you.

Do I need to pay any registration or listing fees?

No, TextNook does not charge any registration or listing fees from any of its sellers. You will only be charged a nominal fee on any transaction you carry out.

How long does it take to receive my shipment?

Most of the sellers on platform process and ship the orders in the first 2-3 days from the date of order and most of the orders will be delivered in 5-7 days from the date of shipment.

However, the time taken for delivery is seller specific and they mention the approximate timelines against the books they are offering on TextNook.

In case of the places, where the reputed national shipping/courier companies do not have coverage, sellers may deliver via India Post and the delivery time could be longer.

Where all do you deliver?

We ship to all parts of India. We do not ship to international destinations.

What do I do if I don’t receive my order on time?

We regret the inconvenience caused because of the undue delay. Please call us at 1-800-200-6353 or drop us an e-mail to service@textnook.in with the details of the order. We will work on tracking the same and getting it delivered to you.

However, kindly note that neither the carrier nor TextNook or its sellers take responsibility for delays caused due to inclement weather or situations that are beyond our control.

How do I track my order?

You can track the shipments using the tracking number provided by the respective carriers. In case of India Post, please note that you may not get the exact details of the status as it gets updated periodically.

Cancellation Policy

• If your order has not been processed and shipped yet, we will cancel the transaction for you and initiate the refund process.

• However, if the order has been shipped already, you have the option of returning, either for an exchange or a full refund.

• Please e-mail us at service@textnook.in or call us at 1-800-200-6353 with the transaction details.

Return Policy

• Most sellers offer a 7-day return policy.

• However, sellers can choose to set their own return policy for the books they offer to sell/rent on TextNook.

• Examine the seller's return policy carefully before you buy or rent the item.

• In case of returns for full refund, the items must be unworn, original condition. It takes 2-3 weeks time for processing the refund.

• If you are returning the book(s) before seven (7) days for a full refund, then the shipping expenses have to be borne by you.
